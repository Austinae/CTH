Confection Textile Hotelier

Who are we?

CONFECTION TEXTILE HOTELIER is a company which has been in business for over 18 years. Based in France, it is specialized in the manufacturing of textile articles. Numerous hotels have already placed their trust in us and are satisfied with the work we have provided, so why not try it for yourself? To view more information about who we are working with I invite you to visit our partners page.

If you have worked with us you will recognize Arnaud Guerrand's amiable face, he is the Head of sales & marketing and most importantly co-founded CTH back in July 2003 along with his associate Ludovic Maillard.

We work tirelessly to provide the best products and customer care and hope that you will join us on this journey. Please do take a look at our gallery page which is a display of images from previous projects and feel free to contact us for more information, thank you!
